Common Questions
Is the 12-week timeline fixed?
No. It is a typical shape, not a rigid contract. Actual scope and pace are agreed with each client at kickoff based on where they are and what they need. The roadmap on this page is indicative.
Do you guarantee a fundraise close?
No. We handle strategy, preparation, and outreach on your behalf — the "15–25 investor conversations" figure is a programme target, not a guaranteed outcome. Fundraising results depend on many factors outside any adviser's control.
Are you a registered investment adviser or stockbroker?
No. UpForge Consulting LLP and Scoutline Capital are consulting and startup advisory firms. Neither is registered as an Investment Adviser under SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013, or as any other regulated intermediary. Services are advisory in nature only.
What industries do you work in?
Across both firms: Security, FnB, Ed-Tech, Jewellery, Logistics, Consumer Goods, and more — 18 industries to date. We'll tell you at the first call if your sector isn't a good fit.
Where are you based?
UpForge Consulting LLP is incorporated in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India (LLPIN: ACR-4016). Scoutline Capital operates at scoutlinecapital.in. Both firms have worked with clients across 6 countries.
