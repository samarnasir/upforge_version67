OUR PROCESS

The UpForge 8–Step Flagship Process
Built For Brands That Are Ready To Move
STEP 1
Business & Founder Context Alignment
Understand founder vision, constraints, and what "winning" looks like.
STEP 2
Market & Competitive Landscape Analysis
Scan trends, threats, saturation, and whitespace.
STEP 3
Customer Intelligence Deep Dive
Extract true pain points through direct user insight.

Strategic Wedge & MVP Offering Design
